I'm Onyeuba Ebuka Godwin — a Product Manager with hands-on experience taking digital and fintech products from concept to launch. My work sits at the intersection of user needs, business goals, and technical execution.

I founded AcedXTrades in 2021, building it into a crypto and gift card exchange platform while managing the full product lifecycle — roadmapping, user research, sprint planning, and cross-functional coordination across engineering, design, marketing, and media teams.

I also designed a teen debit card concept for Kuda Microfinance Bank during my EntryLevel PM programme — a product addressing how minors aged 11–18 can access digital payments without a bank account, supervised by their parents.

Before product management, I worked in legislative operations as Chief of Staff at the National Youth Assembly of Nigeria (Delta State), and as a Radio & TV Presenter at Delta Broadcasting Service — roles that sharpened my communication, stakeholder management, and deadline-driven execution.

I'm open to PM roles across Nigeria, the UK, EU, and remote-first teams. I'm particularly drawn to fintech, digital banking, and products built for the African market.
